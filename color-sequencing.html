Color Sequencing Dialog

This color sequencing widget was developed as a proof of concept to reorder large numbers of content blocks inside a YUI dialog box, specifically testing using the YUI 3 DragDrop library inside a YUI 2.7 application.

Click here to open the sequencing dialog box.

Move one color: Click and drag to move a color into its desired location.

Multiple colors: Hold down the Shift key, click to select more than 1 color, and drag selection.
